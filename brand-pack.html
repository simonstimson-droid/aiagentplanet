06 — Voice & Tone

Brand Voice

Do

  • Conversational — like a smart friend who works in AI
  • Explain WHY things matter, not just WHAT happened
  • Admit when it's a quiet day — don't pad
  • Use plain English, define jargon in brackets
  • Punchy headlines, tight summaries
  • Authoritative but not arrogant

Don't

  • Hype or use superlatives ("revolutionary", "game-changing")
  • Write like a press release
  • Explain things like you're lecturing
  • Use unexplained acronyms or jargon
  • Pad slow days with fluff
  • Be sycophantic about big companies
