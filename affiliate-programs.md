## How to Use in Content

### Daily News
- When a tool is mentioned in news, link to it with affiliate link
- "Tool of the Day" section — always use affiliate link
- Format: `[Tool Name](https://affiliate-link) — what it does`

### Weekly Roundup
- "Tools & Launches" section — affiliate links for any tools mentioned
- "Funding & Deals" — link to the company's site

### Landing Page
- "Recommended Tools" section with affiliate links
- Comparison posts (e.g., "Cursor vs Copilot vs Replit")

## Tracking
- Use UTM parameters: `?utm_source=aiagentplanet&utm_medium=telegram&utm_campaign=daily`
- Keep a spreadsheet of which links perform best
- A/B test different CTA formats

## Revenue Estimates (rough)
- 1,000 subscribers × 5% click-through × 10% conversion × $20 avg commission = ~$100/day

*These are optimistic estimates. Real numbers depend heavily on audience quality and content relevance.*
